Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

The Short Version

Pulse collects minimal data to make the app work — your anonymous vote on each day's question. There are no accounts, no personal profiles, and no way to identify you.

What We Collect

We store an anonymous device ID paired with your vote. This is the minimum required to prevent duplicate votes. We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information.

How We Use It

Your vote is used solely to calculate and display the community result. We aggregate results — we never display individual votes.

Analytics & Tracking

Pulse contains no advertising SDKs or tracking pixels. We may use basic anonymous analytics to improve the app — never tied to your identity.

Third Parties

We use Supabase to store vote data. Supabase is SOC 2 compliant. We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties.

Data Retention

Poll results are kept indefinitely. Your anonymous vote record is retained for as long as the app is live.

Children

Pulse is designed for general audiences. Because we collect no personal information, there is no risk to children's privacy.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use means acceptance.

Contact

Questions? Email us at labs@vobius.com — we're a small team and read every message.
